About

For details and changelog visit our website

Last.fm Scrobbler for Chrome was created for people who listen to music online through their browser, and would like to keep an updated playback history using last.fm's scrobbling service.

By now the Scrobbler only supports YouTube and Napster (experimental)

As I don't visit many other websites with video content, the development of connectors for other websites will be mostly up to you. Feel free to send a pull request at GitHub. Don't worry, even if noone contributes I will still keep YouTube scrobbling working myself.

And since we all love music and Last.fm, you can also stop by at my profile to compare taste or maybe leave a kind message :-)
